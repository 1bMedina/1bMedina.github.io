My Experience

This is some of my experience, in school and outside of school!

NASA ADC Club

Led and founded a diverse group of majority women to accomplish a goal of visualizing lunar terrain data using python.
We built a app for a 3D visualization of thousands of lunar terrain data points (latitude/longitude). We then converted data into Cartesian coordinates, transforming them into PNG formats, loading them into Ursina Game Engine for dynamic rendering. Additionally, we fundraised over $4,000 to support club activities and to fund a trip to PyCon US 2024. Lastly, we were given the opportunity to presented our application at a national python conference in Pittsburgh, PyCon 2024.
